FAQs

Is now a good time to start my home interiors?


Yes, it is! From consultations at our Experience Centres to on-site installation, we follow a strict no-contact safety policy. We ensure everything from temperature checks to fumigation is taken care of, so you can confidently begin your dream project.
Since co-creating a design usually takes 4-6 weeks, you can begin with an online consultation. Our designers are available to assist you via video calls, or you can schedule an appointment to visit our Experience Centre.

Can I meet my interior designer?


Certainly, prioritizing safety, we offer two ways to interact:
1. Online consultation
2. Contactless, safe design meetings at our Experience Centres
Please note: If your location is in a Red or Containment Zone, we can only offer online consultations and will not be able to facilitate face-to-face meetings.

Can I have a meeting at my home?


We recommend meeting at our Experience Centre, which is fully safe for your visit. We are taking all necessary precautions to ensure a secure environment. However, if preferred, you can opt for an online consultation, and we’d be happy to discuss your project in detail.

How can I select materials for design?


While we can share images of materials and finishes during the design phase online, finalizing them will require a visit to our Experience Centre. Rest assured, our centres are safe, and we are following all necessary protocols. Please note, we can only facilitate visits from Green and Orange Zones and will not be able to accommodate visitors who do not adhere to safety guidelines.

How will the measurements of my site be taken?


Please share the floor plan of your home with our designers to kickstart your project. If you don’t have the floor plan, we will assign a representative for a site visit. Rest assured, our representatives will adhere to all safety protocols to ensure both their safety and yours.

What will the timeline be for completing my project?


We will keep you informed about the timelines based on discussions with the designers. The lockdown forced us to pause the handover of all projects, but with the relaxation of restrictions, things are gradually returning to normal. We're doing everything we can to make up for the lost time and ensure the safe delivery of your home interiors.

Can I contact my interior designer for any queries or clarifications?


Yes, absolutely. You can reach out to your designer via video calls or messages, whichever is more convenient for you. We are always here to assist you.
